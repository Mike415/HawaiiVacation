6:15 PM Cliff Dive Ceremony at Black Rock
Free
Every evening at sunset, a traditional torch-lighting and cliff dive ceremony at Pu'u Keka'a (Black Rock). A performer lights torches along the cliff, chants, then dives into the ocean below. Free, kid-friendly, and one of the most iconic Maui traditions.
Gather on the beach around 6pm. Walk south from Hula Grill after dinner — it's right along the beachfront path. Jordan will love watching the torch-lighting. Honu (sea turtles) often rest on the sand nearby too.
📍 Black Rock (Pu'u Keka'a)
